  ## What data we collect and why

  ### Conversation data
  When you chat with Ory, your messages are sent to Anthropic (the makers of
  Claude AI) via a secure proxy server to generate responses. Conversations are
  stored on your device and synced via iCloud. We do not store your conversations
  on our own servers.

  ### Mood logs
  Your daily mood entries are stored on your device and synced via iCloud only.

  ### Your name and profile
  If you provide your name, it is stored on your device and iCloud. Ory builds a
  brief profile from your conversations to provide continuity across sessions.
  This profile is stored on your device and iCloud only.

  ### Purchase data
  If you subscribe to Ory Plus, your purchase is processed by Apple and tracked
  by RevenueCat. RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier and subscription
  status — not your name or contact details.
  See: [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)
